Matthew T. Regehr

Hi! I am a master's student in the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I am grateful to be advised by Gautam Kamath and Shai Ben-David and will begin PhD studies in the Fall 2023 semester.

My research interests are broadly machine learning theory and differential privacy. My current research projects focus on theory and algorithms for differentially private accounting.
